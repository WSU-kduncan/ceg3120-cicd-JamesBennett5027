Introduction

Persona 5 is a Japanese role-playing video game developed by Atlus. It is the sixth main entry in the Persona series, a spinoff of Atlus' larger Shin Megami Tensei franchise. Released in 2016 in Japan and in 2017 worldwide, the game follows a group of high school students who become Phantom Thieves of Hearts, using their powers to change the hearts of corrupt adults in society.

Characters

Persona 5 features a cast of diverse and memorable characters, each with their unique personas and motivations. Some of the key characters include:

  • Joker (Protagonist) - The silent protagonist who becomes the leader of the Phantom Thieves.
  • Ryuji Sakamoto - A rebellious and hot-headed former track star, Ryuji becomes one of the first members of the Phantom Thieves.
  • Ann Takamaki - A model student with a passion for helping those in need, Ann is another key member of the Phantom Thieves.
  • Makoto Niijima - A disciplined and intelligent student council president who joins the team later in the game.
  • Futaba Sakura - A genius hacker who supports the Phantom Thieves from behind the scenes.

Plot

The plot of Persona 5 revolves around a high school student who is falsely accused of a crime and forced to transfer to Shujin Academy. While attending school, the protagonist and his friends discover a mysterious alternate world called the Metaverse. They gain the ability to enter the Metaverse and change the hearts of corrupt adults by stealing their distorted desires. Throughout their journey, the Phantom Thieves of Hearts uncover a greater conspiracy that threatens society itself.

Gameplay

Persona 5 combines turn-based combat with life simulation elements. Players take on the role of the protagonist and attend school, build relationships, and participate in various activities during the day. By night, players explore the Metaverse and fight shadows in dungeons.
